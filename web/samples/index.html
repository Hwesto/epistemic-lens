The Same Story — design samples

Seven card archetypes. The daily selector picks ONE based on what's most striking in today's analysis. Open any of these in a browser to preview.

  1. Word card Six countries, six words for the same event. Translations under each in italics. Default fallback — always available from within-language LLR.
  2. Paradox card Two opposing-bloc outlets, same conclusion. Pulled from analysis.paradox detection. Rare (~1 in 4 days).
  3. Silence card One country didn't cover something everyone else did. Pulled from coverage_matrix (4-state) + silence detection.
  4. Shift card One bucket's frame shifts over five days. Pulled from longitudinal trajectory. Needs 5+ days of story history.
  5. Sources card Whose voices an outlet platformed today, broken down by speaker type. Pulled from sources.schema + PR 3's speaker_affiliation enrichment. Daily.
  6. Tilt card Outlets whose vocabulary drifted most this week, triangulated against two anchors (wire + cross-bucket-mean). Pulled from tilt.schema + PR 7's second anchor. Weekly.
  7. Echo card Outlet A's headline, then outlet B's 48 hours later. Pulled from weekly cross_outlet_lag CCF. Mondays only.
