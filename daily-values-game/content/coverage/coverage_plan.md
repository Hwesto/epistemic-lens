# Coverage plan вҖ” the corpus as a designed experiment

> The stories **are** the instrument. A pile of good stories tagged after the fact
> is anecdotes; a planned corpus is a test. **Plan the structure upfront; leave the
> taxonomy open.** Build a corpus *structurally capable of proving the framework wrong* (В§5).

## The spine to cover

Validated axes (MFQ-2): `care`, `equality`, `proportionality`, `loyalty`,
`authority`, `purity`. Probe extras (`honesty`, `autonomy`) **lightly**.

## Targets (illustrative maths from В§5)

| Milestone                          | Stories | What it buys |
|------------------------------------|---------|--------------|
| Touch every spine edge once        | ~6вҖ“7    | A first pass over the space |
| Each edge ~5Г— (a baseline)         | ~30вҖ“40  | Enough to *measure* an edge once; tells discriminating items from dead ones |
| + scope/framing crossings + 3-way binds | ~150вҖ“200 | A *recalibratable* picture across contexts |

~5 gates/story вүҲ 5 edges per story. Track `done_reps` per edge against
`target_reps` via the `coverage` view; the gaps show themselves.

## Variation across re-runs (the story is shared, so vary over TIME)

Because everyone gets the same story on a given day, you vary the *probe* across
*dates*, not within a fork. For a given edge, schedule re-runs that change:

- **scope_variant**: `kin` вҶ’ `stranger` вҶ’ `group` вҶ’ `humanity`
- **framing_variant**: `identifiable_victim` вҶ’ `loss` вҶ’ `gain` вҶ’ `neutral`
- **process_frame**: `rule` vs `outcome`

Comparing the same edge across framings is what turns framing-sensitivity into
*signal* (a measurable confound number) instead of noise (В§7).

## Leave the taxonomy open

- Reserve **exploratory slots** (`is_exploratory = true`) staging tensions
  *outside* the assumed axes. If every item is engineered to load exactly one
  planned axis, analysis can only rediscover your design.
- Target ~10вҖ“15% of gates as exploratory in the early corpus.

## Discipline: rich setup, clean fork

Pour immersion into the **scene**; keep the 2вҖ“4 **options** engineered to stage
exactly one trade-off. You cannot tag your way to a clean fork after writing a
mushy one. Richness leaking into the *options* is what wrecks categorisation.

## The first ~30 stories

A hand-authored coverage sweep, **anchors planted from day one**. After that,
Opus drafts more; a human still curates and tags every single one.
